Let us now see a small description about the 8 major cricketing countries across the world
1.Australia
Australia is one of the most successful cricketing country in the world.It has won 5 ODI world cups along with 1 T20 world cup.The team has been so consistent and strong for many years.World class players like Ricky ponting,Steve Smith,Michael clarke,Brett Lee,Shane Warne are from this country
2.England
This is the country from where the cricket originated.England is undoubtedly one of the best teams in cricket.They are the latest ODI world cup winners but surprisingly they have won only once.World class players such as Alastair cook,Kevin petersen,Andrew Strauss,Ben Stokes,Joe root are from this country
3.India
India has now emerged has one of the top teams in world cricket.The major milestones in Indian cricket is 1983 world cup win along with 2011 ODI world cup.The team has also won 2007 T20 cricket world cup. It is one of the most innovative team in the world.World class players like Sunil Gavaskar,Kapil Dev,Sourav Ganguly,Sachin Tendulkar,Anil Kumble,MS Dhoni,Virat kohli are from this team
4.New Zealand
New Zealand cricket team is also among the top 5 best teams in the world.Though it has not yet won a single world cup title,this team has been so consistent with its performance.The team missed the world cup win last year by a small margin.World class players like Kane Williamson,Daniel Vettori,Brendon Mccullum,Stephen Flemming,Glenn Turner,Trenn boult are from this team
5.South Africa
South africa cricket team is one of the top class teams and consistent teams in world cricket.The irony is that South Africa has not even won a single World Cup title.This is a big surprising thing.World class players like Ab De Velliers,Jonty Rhodes,Graeme smith,Shaun Pollock,Jacques Kallis,Dale Steyn belong to this team.
6.Pakistan
Pakistan is also among the top 8 teams in the world.This team is undoubtedly a very aggressive and a consistent team.As always their most favourite opponent is India.They have won ODI World cup in 1992 along with the latest Champions trophy title 2017.World class players like Wasim Akram,Shahid Afridi,Babar Azam,Imran Khan,Waqar younis,Misbah-Ul-Haq belong this team.
7.Sri Lanka
Sri lanka cricket team has done well for many years.It has won ODI world cup in 1996 and T20 world cup 2014.They have not been a consistently good team.World class players like Sanath Jayasuriya,Kumar Sangakara,Lasith Malinga,Mahela Jayawardane,Muttiah Muralidharan,Tilakaratne Dilshan belong to this team
8.West Indies
West Indies comprises of islands of Cuba,Jamaica and Puerto Rica.West Indies won the first 2 ODI World cups hels in cricket.They have also won T20 World cups in 2012 and 2016 .The team has been not a good consistent team in ODI now though they perform brilliantly in T20.World class players like Brian Lara,Clive Lloyd,George Hadley,Viv Richards,Chris Gayle,Kieron Pollard,Andre Russell belong to this team
9.Bangladesh
Bangladesh is one of the emerging teams in world cricket.It has been improving steadily in the recent years.Recently it had qualified for world cup 2020 and has won its 1st trophy in ICC under-19 Cricket World Cup.Famous players like Shakib AL hasan,Mashraife Mortaza,Mustafizur Rahman,Mushfaqir Rahman belong to this team.
10.Afghanistan
Afghanistan is another emerging team from Asia.It has seen a good rise in cricket.This team has been improving year by year.One of the main strengths of this team has been bowling.Famous players like Rashid Khan,Mujeeb Ul Rahman,Mohammad Nabi,Gulbadin Naib belong to this team.
